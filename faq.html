RESCHEDULING, CANCELLATIONS, AND REFUNDS

I totally get it: emergencies and changes in circumstances (and your mind) happen... Hopefully, by having these laid out before you pay prepares you in case you do need to reschedule or cancel!

If you have any questions or do need to change/cancel your reading, please send me an email.

Re-scheduling your appointment :

If you need to re-schedule your live reading, please let me know at least 24 hours in advance. You will have priority when re-scheduling for up to two weeks.

To re-schedule, please email me at tarot@jlowatari.com.

Canceling your appointment :

If you need to cancel your live reading, please let me know at least 24 hours in advance.

If you need to cancel your email reading before expected turnaround has passed (currently 7 days), please let me know ASAP. If it's because of an emergency or financial situation, I would be happy to speak to you about other payment options.

Refunds are subject to fees--for now this only applies to PayPal (but is subject to change) as each transaction sent to me via Paypal gets 2.9% + 30 cents taken out.

To cancel, please email me at tarot@jlowatari.com.

Refund Policy :

Live Readings :
Unfortunately I cannot offer refunds for same-day 24 hours) cancellations or no-shows for live readings.

Email Readings :
I can only offer refunds for email readings AFTER the expected turnaround time has passed. (Currently that is after one week / 7 days.) For other situations, please refer to the "Cancelling your appointment" section.
